Why a plastic keg?

Plastic beer kegs are recycleable kegs fabricated from nylon and polyethelyne teraphthelate (PET) plastic.


Why plastic beer kegs?

Plastic beer kegs are lighter; breweries can save up to 65% in transportation cost1.
